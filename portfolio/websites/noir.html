§ Chapter IV — The atelier

A maison built on taste, kept at human scale.

NŌIR was founded in 2009 by Céline Moreau, a former colour chemist, in a narrow workshop behind Mont Thabor. It opened as a single boutique with eleven brands and one rule: if we don't use it ourselves, we don't sell it.

Sixteen years later, the rule has not changed. We now carry eighty-six houses across parfum, soin, couleur, cheveux and corps — and pour our own atelier collection of twelve lip shades, by hand, in the same workshop.

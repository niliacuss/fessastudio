01
The workshop

Forged in Porto,
by 14 hands.

Our bottles are made by a team of fourteen at a family-run factory on the edge of the Douro river. They've been working steel since 1987.

Every bottle is hand-checked, pressure-tested, and signed with the initials of whoever assembled it. You'll find them inside the cap.

Azores, NLOcean cleanup partner.
02
1% pledge

Every bottle cleans
the ocean a bit.

One percent of every sale goes to The Ocean Cleanup. That means every bottle removes — on average — three plastic items from the North Pacific gyre.

In 2024 we funded the removal of 46,200 kg of plastic. In 2025, we'd like to double that.
